橙子铃声 Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

橙子铃声 ("we", "us", or "the App") is an iOS tool for importing audio, trimming clips, and exporting ringtones. By using the App, you agree to this policy.

Information We Collect

We only process information you actively provide: media files you import, edits you make (trim range, title, export status), and technical data necessary to run the App on your device. We do not require account creation and do not collect government IDs or payment information.

How We Use Information

  • Import and process audio for ringtone creation
  • Preview and export .m4r files
  • Remember your projects on this device

We do not sell your personal data.

Photo Library

When you choose Album, iOS may ask for photo library access so you can select a video. We only access videos you explicitly select. Selected content is copied to the App sandbox for local audio extraction and is not transmitted to our servers.

Local Storage

Imported media, project metadata, and exported ringtones are stored locally in the App container on your device. You can delete individual items in the App or remove all App data by deleting the App from your iPhone.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. Your exported ringtone is shared only when you explicitly use the iOS share sheet. We may disclose information if required by law.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your region). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your Rights

You may deny or revoke photo access in iOS Settings, delete projects inside the App, uninstall the App to remove local data, or contact us with privacy questions.
